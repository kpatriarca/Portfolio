AllowanceSense

AllowanceSense is a smart financial planning web application designed to help students and young adults manage their allowances, track daily expenses, and develop responsible spending habits. The platform addresses common challenges faced by students such as overspending, lack of budgeting discipline, and poor financial awareness. By offering real-time tracking, automated calculations, and visual summaries, AllowanceSense encourages financial responsibility, improved money management, and long-term financial literacy.
